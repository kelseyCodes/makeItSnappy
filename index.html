INSTRUCTIONS


1) Go to a quiet place. No talking!
2) Turn your sound on mute.
3) When prompted, allow Chrome access to your microphone and camera.
4) When the bird is over the nest, snap your fingers to get 10 points! Wrong snaps result in negative points.
5) Wave your hand left and right to move your nest and catch the bird. A wave up will move the nest back to the middle. Tip: make sure your camera is about a foot and a half from your hand.
